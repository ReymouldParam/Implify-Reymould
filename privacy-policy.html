    Privacy Policy

    Last Updated: Feb 5, 2025

    Introduction

    One of the business lines of Implify Inc (“Implify“ or “we”) is to provide staffing and recruiting services, including temporary and permanent job placement. We prioritize the protection of personal and confidential information of our clients, candidates, and employees. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal data in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

    Scope

    This Privacy Policy applies to all personal data collected by Implify Technologies, including:

    • Candidates registering for job opportunities
    • Clients engaging our staffing and recruiting services
    • Employees working with us or on behalf of clients
    • Visitors to our website (https://implifyinc.com)

    Types of Personal Data Collected

    • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, physical address
    • Professional Information: Work experience, skills, education, certifications
    • Job Search Information: Job preferences, applications, resume, cover letter
    • Employment History: Employment dates, job titles, employer names
    • Financial Information: Payment details (for invoicing only)

    How We Collect Personal Data

    We collect personal data through:

    • Online forms on our website
    • Phone and email communications
    • In-person interactions, including interviews and meetings

    How We Use Personal Data

    • To match candidates with job openings
    • To provide staffing and recruiting services
    • To communicate job opportunities and application statuses
    • To process payments
    • To maintain accurate records
    • To improve our services

    Opt-Out Process

    You may stop receiving text messages from Implify Technologies at any time by replying with “STOP” or “UNSUBSCRIBE” to any message, or by sending your request to (302) 533 2345. We will promptly remove your number from our messaging list.

    How We Disclose Personal Data

    • To government authorities, as required by law
    • To third parties assisting in service delivery (e.g., platform providers, phone companies)

    Security Measures

    • Encryption of sensitive data
    • Secure servers and databases
    • Limited access to personal data
    • Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures

    Data Retention

    We retain personal data as long as necessary for business purposes or as required by law. Once no longer needed, we delete or anonymize the data.

    Rights of Individuals

    Individuals have the right to:

    • Request access to their personal data
    • Request corrections or erasure of their personal data
    • Object to processing of personal data for specific purposes
    • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

    SMS Consent

    All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties. We will not share your opt-in to an SMS campaign with any third party for purposes unrelated to providing you with the services of that campaign. We may share your Personal Data, including your SMS opt-in or consent status, with third parties that help us provide our messaging services, including but not limited to platform providers, phone companies, and any other vendors who assist us in the delivery of text messages.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Updates will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
