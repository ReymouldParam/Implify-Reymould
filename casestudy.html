RFID Implementation for a GPS Services Provider

The client is a leading GPS Equipment provider to individual and corporate customers across the US.

Business Challenge:

The client procures equipment from several suppliers and stores them in three warehouses. Their ever-changing Model configuration and Customer Order configuration pose a significant problem in tracking several parts within the organization and configuring them for Customers.

Our Solution & RFID Benefits:

Implify has devised a strategy based on Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) techniques to provide the following benefits:

  • Speed Up Asset Tracking Process
  • Improve Accuracy
  • Locate Assets Easily
  • Timely Order Deliveries
  • Track IT Related Information
  • Maintain Insurance Records
  • Comply with Government Regulations
  • Meet Audit Requirements
  • Improve Depreciation Reporting
  • Minimize Paperwork
  • Create One Central Asset Repository
  • Keep History of Asset Use
